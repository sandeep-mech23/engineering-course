MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

Definition and Scope:

Mechanical engineering is a versatile branch of engineering that focuses on the design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems and devices Mechanical engineering is the study of physical machines and mechanisms, integrating principles from physics, mathematics, and materials science. It encompasses a wide range of applications, including the design and development of engines, machines, and various mechanical systems. This field is one of the oldest and broadest engineering disciplines, playing a crucial role in industries such as automotive, aerospace, energy, manufacturing, and robotics.
