ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING

Definition and Scope:

Electrical engineering is a branch of engineering focused on the study, design, and application of systems and devices that use electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. Definition and Scope Electrical engineering emerged as a distinct field in the late 19th century, primarily due to advancements in electrical power generation, telecommunications, and the electric telegraph.
