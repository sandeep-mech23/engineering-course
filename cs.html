COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERING

Definition and Scope:

Computer Science Engineering (CSE) is a discipline that combines principles of computer science and engineering to design, develop, and manage computer systems and software applications.Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is an academic subject comprising approaches of computer science and computer engineering. There is no clear division in computing between science and engineering, just like in the field of materials science and engineering. However, some classes are historically more related to computer science (e.g. data structures and algorithms), and other to computer engineering (e.g. computer architecture). CSE is also a term often used in Europe to translate the name of technical or engineering informatics academic programs. It is offered in both undergraduate as well postgraduate with specializations.
